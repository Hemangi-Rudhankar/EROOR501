CARBON FOOTPRINT CALCULATOR

         A carbon footprint calculator is a tool that helps individuals, businesses, or organizations estimate the amount of carbon dioxide and other greenhouse gas emissions produced by their activities. These calculators take into account factors such as energy use, transportation, diet, and waste generation.

         There are several online carbon footprint calculators available that can provide estimates for individuals or households. These calculators typically ask for information such as the size of the household, the types of vehicles used, the frequency of air travel, and the types of food consumed. The calculator then generates an estimate of the individual or household's carbon footprint, usually measured in metric tons of carbon dioxide equivalent (CO2e).
